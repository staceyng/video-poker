Video Poker

How to play video poker

1. Bet is fixed at 5 credits
2. Hit Start button to receive your initial five-card poker hand
3. Discard card you don't deem useful, refer to scoring table
4. Receive replacement cards for up to 5 discarded cards
5. Winnings are paid out according to the score table
Hand Score Example
